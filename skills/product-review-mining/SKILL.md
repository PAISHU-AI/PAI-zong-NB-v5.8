# product-review-mining

## Purpose

Use review language to improve product messaging, FAQ, creative angles, and product roadmap notes.

## Workflow

1. Collect provided reviews, Q&A, support notes, or competitor feedback.
2. Group themes: praised benefits, objections, confusion, usage context, comparison points, and unmet needs.
3. Convert themes into PDP copy, ad hooks, FAQ, creative briefs, and product improvement notes.
4. Mark evidence strength based on sample size and source quality.
5. Avoid exposing private or sensitive customer details.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate reviews or sentiment counts.
- Do not include direct personal data.
- If data is too small, label findings as directional.

## Output

Return theme table, buyer language snippets, messaging angles, FAQ ideas, and product action notes.
