# product-comparison-chart

## Purpose

Help shoppers choose between variants, bundles, competitors, or product tiers with clear comparison content.

## Workflow

1. Identify products, variants, buyer needs, comparison dimensions, and proof sources.
2. Choose table structure: variant chooser, good-better-best, competitor comparison, bundle comparison, or use-case matrix.
3. Write short benefit-led labels and decision guidance.
4. Flag missing or unverified facts.
5. Provide copy for PDP, A+ Content, landing page, or sales deck use.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate competitor facts.
- Avoid unfair or unverifiable comparison claims.
- Keep table dimensions useful, not decorative.

## Output

Return comparison table, recommended buyer path, copy notes, and fact gaps.
