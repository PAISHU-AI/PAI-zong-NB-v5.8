# shoppable-video-strategy

## Purpose

Design short-form and live-commerce videos where the viewer can move naturally from interest to product action.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, product, audience, buying trigger, offer, and product-action path.
2. Choose format: short demo, problem-solution, comparison, UGC, bundle, live selling clip, or FAQ.
3. Place product mentions, CTA, proof, and tag moments without overwhelming the viewer.
4. Write script beats with time marks.
5. Add measurement plan for view-to-action and conversion indicators.

## Required Checks

- Do not imply platform actions were configured unless verified.
- Keep offer, pricing, scarcity, and guarantee claims factual.
- Ask before account actions or external posting.

## Output

Return format choice, timed script, CTA map, product-tag plan, production notes, and KPI checklist.
