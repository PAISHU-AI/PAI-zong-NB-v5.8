# chat-commerce-script

## Purpose

Create helpful chat flows that guide shoppers to the right product and answer buying questions.

## Workflow

1. Identify product catalog, shopper needs, support boundaries, recommendation rules, and escalation points.
2. Draft opening questions, qualification flow, recommendation copy, objection responses, and close.
3. Add fallback replies for missing product fit, policy questions, and support handoff.
4. Include tone and safety rules.
5. Mark data or integration requirements.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim chat automation was deployed.
- Do not invent inventory, delivery, warranty, or return policy details.
- Escalate sensitive support or account issues.

## Output

Return chat flow, scripts, decision rules, fallback messages, and implementation notes.
