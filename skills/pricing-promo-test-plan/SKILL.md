# pricing-promo-test-plan

## Purpose

Create a practical test plan for pricing and promotions while separating assumptions from confirmed business data.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, price, margin constraints, traffic source, order value, and promotion goals.
2. Choose test type: price, coupon, free shipping threshold, bundle, gift-with-purchase, or limited offer.
3. Define hypothesis, audience group, pages/messages affected, and guardrails.
4. List required data and decision criteria.
5. Add risks for margin, brand positioning, and operational complexity.

## Required Checks

- Do not give financial advice or guarantee lift.
- Do not invent margins, conversion rates, or customer data.
- Do not recommend deceptive scarcity.

## Output

Return test matrix, hypotheses, copy implications, data needs, and risk notes.
