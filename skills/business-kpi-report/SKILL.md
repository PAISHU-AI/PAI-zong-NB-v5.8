# business-kpi-report

## Purpose

Turn operating metrics into a concise report with decisions and follow-up actions.

## Workflow

1. Identify business area, period, audience, KPI definitions, targets, and prior comparison.
2. Summarize current performance, trend, variance, and drivers.
3. Separate metric facts from interpretation.
4. Add risks, opportunities, and recommended actions.
5. Include missing data or measurement caveats.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent KPIs, targets, or performance values.
- Do not imply causality without evidence.
- Keep action items tied to observed metrics.

## Output

Return KPI table, executive summary, variance commentary, risks/opportunities, and action list.
