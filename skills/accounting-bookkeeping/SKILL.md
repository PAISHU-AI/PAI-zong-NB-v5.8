# accounting-bookkeeping

## Purpose

Organize basic bookkeeping work into clear entries, categories, and review steps.

## Workflow

1. Identify entity, period, currency, accounting basis, source records, and chart of accounts.
2. Classify transactions by account, counterparty, tax/fee indicator, and evidence source.
3. Draft journal entries or voucher notes with debit, credit, amount, date, and memo.
4. Reconcile totals and mark missing receipts, duplicate records, or unclear categories.
5. Provide review questions for a qualified accountant when needed.

## Required Checks

- Do not provide legal, tax, or audit assurance.
- Do not invent amounts, invoices, receipts, or account codes.
- Keep every entry traceable to provided evidence or mark it as a placeholder.

## Output

Return journal-entry draft, ledger/category table, exceptions list, reconciliation notes, and accountant-review questions.
