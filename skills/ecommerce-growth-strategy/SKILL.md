# ecommerce-growth-strategy

## Purpose

Find the highest-leverage growth path for an ecommerce business without scattering effort across every possible channel.

## Workflow

1. Capture business stage, product category, channels, revenue, margin, traffic, conversion, AOV, repeat rate, and constraints.
2. Diagnose the growth bottleneck across traffic, conversion, AOV, retention, and expansion.
3. Rank opportunities by impact, confidence, effort, and risk.
4. Build a 90-day roadmap with quick wins, experiments, and required metrics.
5. Add channel-specific notes for Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, TikTok Shop, or DTC when relevant.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent metrics. Ask for missing numbers or mark assumptions.
- Do not present strategy as guaranteed financial outcome.
- Separate analysis, recommendations, and experiments.

## Output

Return diagnosis, top growth lever, prioritized roadmap, experiment backlog, KPIs, and assumptions.
