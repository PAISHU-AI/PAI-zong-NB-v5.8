# xhs-zhongcao-copywriting

## Purpose

Create platform-native Xiaohongshu copy that sounds like a real recommendation instead of a product manual.

## Workflow

1. Identify note type: seed recommendation, shop visit, tutorial, comparison, review, or list.
2. Extract the audience, product or topic, real experience, proof points, forbidden claims, and desired action.
3. Write five title options under 20 Chinese characters where possible.
4. Write short mobile paragraphs with a conversational tone, restrained emoji, and concrete details.
5. Add 8-15 tags across broad category, vertical niche, audience, scenario, and long-tail search terms.
6. Add image-card suggestions for cover and inner pages.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate product use, medical effects, financial claims, scarcity, discounts, or testimonials.
- Do not write banned, deceptive, or compliance-sensitive claims.
- Keep paragraphs short enough for phone reading.
- Make the note feel personal, specific, and useful.

## Output

Return title options, final note body, tags, cover idea, inner-card ideas, and a short quality checklist.
