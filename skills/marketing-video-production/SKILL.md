# marketing-video-production

## Purpose

Create production-ready plans for marketing video without assuming rendering tools are available.

## Workflow

1. Identify goal, audience, platform, duration, product promise, CTA, and assets.
2. Choose video type: explainer, product demo, UGC, ad, tutorial, testimonial-style, or launch teaser.
3. Write storyboard, script, voice direction, on-screen text, motion notes, and shot list.
4. Choose execution path: prompt-only, existing video edit, programmatic video, or external generator.
5. Define QA for pacing, claims, readability, safe areas, and brand consistency.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim rendered media without evidence.
- Ask before paid generation, external posting, or account actions.
- Keep claims and testimonials factual.

## Output

Return concept, script, storyboard, prompts, production checklist, and validation notes.
