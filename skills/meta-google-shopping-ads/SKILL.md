# meta-google-shopping-ads

## Purpose

Create product-led ad copy and creative variants for paid social and shopping campaigns.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, offer, target buyer, objections, platform, and landing destination.
2. Draft angle families: problem, comparison, social proof, offer, seasonal, bundle, and retargeting.
3. Write short primary text, headline, description, CTA, and creative notes.
4. Add variants for cold, warm, and returning shoppers.
5. Mark policy-sensitive or unverified claims.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim ads were launched or approved.
- Do not invent ROAS, CPC, conversion rate, or audience data.
- Keep regulated claims cautious.

## Output

Return ad copy table, creative notes, angle map, claim risks, and test plan.
