# ecommerce-product-photography

## Purpose

Plan product photos that improve trust, clarity, and conversion across ecommerce listings.

## Workflow

1. Identify product category, platform, target buyer, brand tone, and required listing slots.
2. Define photo sequence: hero, scale, detail, use case, lifestyle, comparison, packaging, and trust proof.
3. Recommend lighting, background, props, framing, and retouching direction.
4. Write image-generation prompts only when the user wants synthetic visuals.
5. Add marketplace compliance and accessibility notes.

## Required Checks

- Use real product attributes when available.
- Do not represent synthetic images as real product photos.
- Avoid misleading scale, ingredients, outcomes, or certifications.

## Output

Return shot list, visual direction, prompts or photographer brief, listing order, and QA checklist.
