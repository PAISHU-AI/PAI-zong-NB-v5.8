# post-purchase-retention

## Purpose

Improve repeat purchase and customer satisfaction after the first order.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, customer stage, order context, repeat-purchase path, and support risks.
2. Plan sequence: thank you, usage tips, support, review request, cross-sell, replenishment, loyalty, winback.
3. Draft email/SMS copy and subject lines.
4. Add timing assumptions and personalization fields.
5. Mark review, incentive, and claim compliance risks.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim messages were sent.
- Do not pressure for reviews in a misleading way.
- Do not invent loyalty terms or replenishment cycles.

## Output

Return retention flow, message copy, cross-sell logic, timing assumptions, and compliance notes.
