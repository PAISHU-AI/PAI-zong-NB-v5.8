# monthly-business-review

## Purpose

Create a monthly business review that connects metrics, narrative, and actions.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, month, business goals, KPI set, prior period, and key initiatives.
2. Summarize performance, wins, misses, drivers, risks, and decisions needed.
3. Build report or slide structure: executive summary, KPI table, trends, deep dives, actions, and appendix.
4. Add chart and table recommendations.
5. Mark missing data and owner follow-ups.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent performance metrics or initiative outcomes.
- Keep commentary evidence-backed.
- Separate report outline from generated file creation when office tools are unavailable.

## Output

Return MBR outline, KPI narrative, chart/table plan, decision log, risks, and action owners.
