# product-launch-campaign

## Purpose

Coordinate launch assets so a new product has coherent messaging across channels.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, launch goal, target buyer, channels, deadline, assets, and constraints.
2. Define launch positioning, offer, proof, objections, and creative angles.
3. Build channel asset list: PDP, images, video, email, SMS, social, creator brief, ads, and FAQ.
4. Create production schedule with dependencies.
5. Add launch QA and claim checklist.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim assets were published or campaigns were launched.
- Do not invent launch metrics.
- Confirm price, offer, stock, and claims before final copy.

## Output

Return launch plan, channel asset matrix, timeline, messaging map, and QA checklist.
