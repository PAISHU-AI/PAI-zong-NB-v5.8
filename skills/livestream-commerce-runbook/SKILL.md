# livestream-commerce-runbook

## Purpose

Plan a live commerce session that keeps product demos, offers, and buyer questions organized.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, product lineup, host, duration, offer, and audience.
2. Build run-of-show: opening, product blocks, proof, demo, objections, offer, FAQ, close.
3. Write host prompts, transition lines, chat prompts, and CTA timing.
4. Plan replay clips and short-form repurposing.
5. Add operational checklist for assets, pricing, stock, and approval.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim a live session was scheduled or started.
- Do not invent price, stock, guarantee, or product availability.
- External store/account actions require confirmation.

## Output

Return runbook, host script, product order, CTA timing, asset checklist, and replay plan.
