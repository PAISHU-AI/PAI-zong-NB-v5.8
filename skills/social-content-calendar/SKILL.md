# social-content-calendar

## Purpose

Turn a product, brand, article, launch, or idea into a practical social content plan.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, audience, brand voice, content goal, assets, and posting cadence.
2. Build content pillars and format mix.
3. Generate post ideas, hooks, captions, short-video scripts, and repurposing paths.
4. Create a calendar with priority, effort, and expected role in the funnel.
5. Add measurement and iteration notes.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate metrics, endorsements, or customer stories.
- Keep platform tone distinct; do not reuse the same text everywhere.
- For Xiaohongshu-specific copy, prefer `xhs-zhongcao-copywriting`.

## Output

Return content pillars, calendar table, post drafts, scripts, hashtags, and testing notes.
