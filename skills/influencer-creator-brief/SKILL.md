# influencer-creator-brief

## Purpose

Prepare a clear creator brief that gives direction without over-controlling the creator voice.

## Workflow

1. Identify campaign goal, product, audience, platform, deliverables, and approval process.
2. Define must-say facts, avoid claims, creative angles, format examples, and CTA.
3. Add product handling, visual requirements, disclosure reminders, and review criteria.
4. Separate mandatory points from optional inspiration.
5. Provide a compact creator-facing version.

## Required Checks

- Do not bypass disclosure or platform rules.
- Do not script fake personal experience.
- Do not imply outreach or contracts were sent.

## Output

Return creator brief, deliverable checklist, claim guardrails, review rubric, and creator-facing summary.
