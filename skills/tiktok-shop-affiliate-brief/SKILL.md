# tiktok-shop-affiliate-brief

## Purpose

Create TikTok Shop creator instructions that support sales while staying clear about product facts and platform actions.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, offer, audience, creator type, and desired video format.
2. Define demo moments, product tag timing, proof points, objection answers, and CTA.
3. Write creator talking points and hook ideas.
4. Add prohibited claims and review checklist.
5. Provide deliverable specs and feedback rubric.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim affiliate setup, commission changes, or product tagging was configured.
- Do not fabricate creator performance or platform eligibility.
- Keep claims factual and reviewable.

## Output

Return affiliate brief, hooks, demo beats, talking points, CTA map, and review rubric.
