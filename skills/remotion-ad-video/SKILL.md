# remotion-ad-video

## Purpose

Turn a product link, app listing, landing page, or product brief into an editable ad-video plan and project contract.

## Workflow

1. Identify source type, product promise, audience, CTA, format, and asset rights.
2. Create an ad brief with assumptions, blockers, output format, and creative route.
3. Build a storyboard with scenes, duration, on-screen text, visuals, motion, and audio notes.
4. Choose Remotion, Hyperframes, or prompt-only fallback based on project stack and available tools.
5. Define render props or variables for repeatable production.
6. Add QA checks for claims, readability, timing, safe areas, and asset provenance.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim video files were rendered without local evidence.
- Do not use third-party assets unless rights are clear.
- Keep product claims supportable.
- External publishing or paid generation requires confirmation.

## Output

Return ad brief, storyboard, asset list, render variables, implementation path, and QA checklist.
