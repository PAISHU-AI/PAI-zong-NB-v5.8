# pdp-conversion-copy

## Purpose

Turn product facts into a clear product detail page that answers purchase questions quickly.

## Workflow

1. Gather product facts, target shopper, objections, proof, price context, and offer.
2. Write hero promise, benefit bullets, feature explanations, FAQ, and trust copy.
3. Align copy with the page structure: above-fold, details, proof, comparison, FAQ, and CTA.
4. Add variants for mobile, short page, and detailed page.
5. Mark unsupported claims as assumptions.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate proof, warranty, scarcity, certifications, or review claims.
- Keep copy scannable and specific.
- If product facts are missing, ask for them or provide placeholders.

## Output

Return page copy, section order, CTA options, FAQ, and claim-risk notes.
