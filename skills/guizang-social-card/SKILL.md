# guizang-social-card

## Purpose

Plan polished Chinese social-card sets with editorial or Swiss-style visual systems.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform: Xiaohongshu, Rednote, WeChat cover, or mixed.
2. Choose visual system: editorial magazine, Swiss grid, product tutorial, or data-method card.
3. Define card count, aspect ratio, cover hierarchy, accent color, typography direction, and image treatment.
4. Convert source material into card-level copy and visual notes.
5. Add implementation direction for HTML, design tool, or image-generation workflow when available.
6. Validate readability, hierarchy, and platform fit.

## Required Checks

- Do not put long article prose directly on cards.
- Keep one strong visual anchor per card.
- Avoid claiming rendered PNG or Live Photo output unless a tool actually produced it.
- For product claims, keep facts and assumptions separate.

## Output

Return visual system, card sequence, copy, layout specs, render prompt or HTML brief, and QA checklist.
