# fal-ugc-video

## Purpose

Create UGC-style video concepts that feel native to social feeds while keeping synthetic or scripted content honest.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, creator persona, audience pain, proof points, and platform.
2. Write hook variants and a 15-60 second script.
3. Define shot sequence, framing, motion, captions, and CTA.
4. Create AI-video or creator-production prompts when relevant.
5. Add compliance notes for claims, sponsorship, and synthetic content.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate real customer testimony.
- Mark synthetic or scripted UGC assumptions.
- Do not claim video generation or posting unless verified.

## Output

Return creator angle, hooks, script, storyboard, prompt, caption, and compliance checklist.
