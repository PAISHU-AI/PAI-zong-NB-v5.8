# seasonal-promo-calendar

## Purpose

Plan promotion timing, assets, offers, and channel messaging around shopping events.

## Workflow

1. Identify market, product lines, dates, offer limits, channels, and production capacity.
2. Build campaign phases: teaser, launch, reminder, last call, post-event, and retention.
3. Define assets per phase for PDP, email, SMS, social, ads, marketplace, and creator content.
4. Add copy hooks and creative themes.
5. Mark assumptions and approval needs.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent discounts, stock, or event participation.
- Do not imply messages or ads were scheduled.
- Keep urgency truthful.

## Output

Return calendar table, phase plan, asset checklist, hooks, and approval notes.
