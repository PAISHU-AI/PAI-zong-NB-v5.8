# cashflow-reporting

## Purpose

Summarize cash movement and near-term liquidity in a format useful for operators.

## Workflow

1. Identify opening cash, period, inflows, outflows, transfers, debt, and one-off items.
2. Group cash activity by operating, investing, financing, or practical business category.
3. Calculate closing cash and runway only when assumptions are provided.
4. Flag timing risks, large payments, receivables, payables, and missing bank evidence.
5. Provide a management-facing summary.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent bank balances, payments, or runway assumptions.
- Do not provide financing or investment advice.
- Mark uncertain timing and missing evidence.

## Output

Return cash movement table, runway view if possible, risk notes, missing data, and management summary.
