# ai-personalization-brief

## Purpose

Define a practical AI personalization or product discovery experience without assuming backend implementation.

## Workflow

1. Identify shopper goal, product catalog, available signals, privacy constraints, and business objective.
2. Choose experience type: quiz, product finder, recommendation, smart search, guided selling, or chat commerce.
3. Define inputs, decision logic, copy, fallback states, and success criteria.
4. List data and integration requirements.
5. Add privacy and transparency checks.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim AI or recommendation systems were deployed.
- Do not use sensitive personal data without explicit basis.
- Do not invent catalog or customer data.

## Output

Return experience brief, flow, copy prompts, data needs, privacy checks, and implementation handoff.
