# amazon-aplus-content

## Purpose

Design Amazon A+ Content that explains the product visually and supports conversion below the fold.

## Workflow

1. Identify product promise, shopper objections, differentiators, and brand voice.
2. Choose A+ module structure: hero, feature grid, lifestyle story, comparison chart, FAQ, and brand block.
3. Write module copy with short headlines and proof-oriented body text.
4. Define image direction for each module.
5. Add review checklist for claims, readability, and consistency with the listing.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim A+ modules were submitted or approved.
- Do not fabricate before/after, award, review, or competitor claims.
- Keep comparison charts factual and sourceable.

## Output

Return module plan, copy blocks, image direction, comparison chart fields, and compliance notes.
