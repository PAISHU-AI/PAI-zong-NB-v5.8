# product-data-feed-optimization

## Purpose

Prepare cleaner product feed content for shopping campaigns, marketplaces, and catalog discovery.

## Workflow

1. Identify feed destination, product category, required fields, optional attributes, and known errors.
2. Review title, description, category, variant, image, price, availability, and attribute consistency.
3. Suggest normalized naming and taxonomy improvements.
4. Create QA checks for missing values, duplicate variants, inconsistent units, and claim risk.
5. Provide CSV-friendly recommendations when needed.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim feed submission or approval.
- Do not invent product identifiers, price, stock, or policy status.
- Treat platform requirements as version-sensitive when not provided.

## Output

Return feed field map, rewrite examples, QA checklist, and CSV/table-ready notes.
