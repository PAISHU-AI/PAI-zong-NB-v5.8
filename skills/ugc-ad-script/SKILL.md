# ugc-ad-script

## Purpose

Create natural creator-style ad scripts that explain the product without sounding like a generic ad.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, audience, platform, creator persona, offer, proof, and key objection.
2. Choose angle: problem-solution, demo, myth, routine, comparison, unboxing, or FAQ.
3. Write hook, beats, visual actions, spoken lines, on-screen text, and CTA.
4. Add alternate hooks and short variants.
5. Mark claims that need proof.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate testimonials, earnings, health results, or review claims.
- Keep creator voice believable and platform-native.
- Do not claim creator posting or ad launch occurred.

## Output

Return script, shot beats, hooks, on-screen text, CTA, and claim notes.
