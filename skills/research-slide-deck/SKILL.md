# research-slide-deck

## Purpose

Turn complex material into a clear presentation structure before slide generation or manual authoring.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, objective, time limit, source material, and desired decision or takeaway.
2. Create narrative arc: context, problem, method, evidence, implications, decision, and next steps.
3. Draft slide-by-slide titles with one message per slide.
4. Add visual suggestions: chart, diagram, table, screenshot, card, or quote.
5. Mark evidence gaps and speaker notes.
6. If slide tooling is available, hand off to a presentation-authoring skill; otherwise output an outline.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent citations, data, or results.
- Keep slide titles message-first.
- Separate speaker notes from on-slide copy.

## Output

Return deck outline, slide table, visual plan, evidence checklist, timing, and handoff notes.
