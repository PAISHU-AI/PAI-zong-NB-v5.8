# tiktok-shop-content-strategy

## Purpose

Plan TikTok Shop content that combines entertainment, product clarity, and conversion intent.

## Workflow

1. Identify product category, audience, price point, creator style, platform constraints, and content goal.
2. Generate content pillars: problem, demo, proof, comparison, story, live teaser, and objections.
3. Write hook patterns and short scripts.
4. Build a 7-30 day content calendar.
5. Add testing matrix for hooks, thumbnails, creators, and CTAs.

## Required Checks

- Do not promise virality or sales outcomes.
- Avoid unverifiable claims and sensitive policy risks.
- Keep scripts short, visual, and product-specific.

## Output

Return content pillars, hooks, scripts, calendar, hashtags, and testing plan.
