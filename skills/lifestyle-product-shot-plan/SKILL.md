# lifestyle-product-shot-plan

## Purpose

Create a lifestyle shot plan that shows the product in believable purchase-driving contexts.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, shopper, use case, environment, constraints, and brand tone.
2. Choose scene concepts that demonstrate scale, use, transformation, or aspiration.
3. Define props, background, angle, lighting, composition, and copy-overlay needs.
4. Produce image-generation prompts or photographer briefs.
5. Add product accuracy and claim checks.

## Required Checks

- Do not alter product features in a misleading way.
- Avoid unrealistic use scenes that conflict with product facts.
- If no visual generation tool is available, deliver prompt/spec only.

## Output

Return scene list, shot briefs, prompts, asset checklist, and accuracy checks.
