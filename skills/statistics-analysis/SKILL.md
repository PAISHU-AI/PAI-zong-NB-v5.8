# statistics-analysis

## Purpose

Analyze data with statistically cautious methods and clear limitations.

## Workflow

1. Identify dataset, grain, sample size, metric definitions, time window, and missing values.
2. Compute descriptive statistics, distributions, groups, correlations, or cohorts as appropriate.
3. Check outliers, denominators, selection bias, and small samples.
4. Interpret results in plain language without overstating causality.
5. Recommend next analysis or validation steps.

## Required Checks

- Do not infer causation from correlation.
- Do not hide small sample size, missing data, or denominator changes.
- Do not fabricate calculations without data.

## Output

Return statistical summary, tables, interpretation, caveats, and next analysis steps.
