# fal-commercial-creative

## Purpose

Plan commercial creative variants that can be generated, edited, or handed to a designer or media tool.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, audience, offer, channel, brand rules, and conversion goal.
2. Choose creative route: product-only, lifestyle, problem-solution, comparison, UGC, demo, or premium brand scene.
3. Write prompt variants with visual constraints, composition, lighting, camera, and negative constraints.
4. Add usage notes for platform aspect ratios and safe areas.
5. Add selection criteria for the best variant.

## Required Checks

- Do not imply real people endorsed the product.
- Do not alter regulated product claims.
- Do not claim generated assets without tool evidence.

## Output

Return creative routes, prompts, variant matrix, production notes, and QA checklist.
