# marketplace-compliance-check

## Purpose

Identify likely content compliance risks before publishing product pages, ads, or marketplace assets.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, category, product, claims, images, offers, and target market.
2. Check claim types: health, safety, earnings, environmental, comparison, guarantee, scarcity, and reviews.
3. Flag risky wording and suggest safer alternatives.
4. List evidence or approvals needed.
5. Separate policy risk from legal advice.

## Required Checks

- Do not provide legal advice.
- Do not guarantee marketplace approval.
- Do not hide risk to make copy more aggressive.

## Output

Return risk table, safer copy options, evidence needs, and approval checklist.
