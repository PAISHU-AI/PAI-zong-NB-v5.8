# email-sms-lifecycle

## Purpose

Design lifecycle messages that move shoppers from first visit to repeat purchase.

## Workflow

1. Identify brand, product, buyer journey, offer, sending channel, and compliance constraints.
2. Choose flow type and message sequence.
3. Draft subject lines, preview text, SMS copy, email body, CTA, and personalization placeholders.
4. Add timing recommendations as planning assumptions.
5. Include compliance and unsubscribe reminders.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim messages were scheduled or sent.
- Do not ignore consent, unsubscribe, or regional rules.
- Do not invent customer data.

## Output

Return flow map, message copy, timing plan, personalization fields, and compliance checklist.
