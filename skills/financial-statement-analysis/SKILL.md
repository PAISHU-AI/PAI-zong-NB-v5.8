# financial-statement-analysis

## Purpose

Turn financial statements into a concise business-performance readout.

## Workflow

1. Identify statement period, currency, accounting basis, business context, and comparison baseline.
2. Summarize revenue, margin, operating cost, profit, assets, liabilities, cash, and working capital.
3. Calculate ratios only when the required fields are present.
4. Separate observed numbers from interpretation and assumptions.
5. Flag anomalies, missing lines, one-off items, and follow-up questions.

## Required Checks

- Do not give investment, tax, or audit advice.
- Do not invent missing statement lines or ratios.
- State data limitations and whether figures are unaudited.

## Output

Return executive summary, metric table, trend notes, risks/anomalies, and follow-up questions.
