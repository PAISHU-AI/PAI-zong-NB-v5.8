# retail-media-ad-creative

## Purpose

Turn ecommerce product facts into marketplace-ready ad creative ideas and copy.

## Workflow

1. Identify platform, product, placement type, audience intent, offer, and constraints.
2. Choose creative angle: search capture, comparison, seasonal need, bundle, repeat purchase, or new launch.
3. Write headline, body copy, image direction, and CTA.
4. Map creative to product page readiness.
5. Add claims and policy review notes.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim campaigns were created or launched.
- Do not fabricate performance metrics or ad eligibility.
- Keep policy-sensitive claims conservative.

## Output

Return ad angles, copy variants, visual direction, PDP readiness notes, and review checklist.
