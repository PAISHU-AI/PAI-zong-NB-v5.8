# marketplace-seo-keywords

## Purpose

Create a practical keyword map for product listings without stuffing copy or inventing search data.

## Workflow

1. Identify product category, shopper intent, use cases, materials, variants, and constraints.
2. Separate keywords into primary, secondary, long-tail, comparison, problem, and occasion groups.
3. Map keywords to title, bullets, body, image alt ideas, FAQ, and backend/search-term fields.
4. Flag terms that need real search-volume validation.
5. Produce a natural-language copy integration plan.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent search volume, ranking, CPC, or competitor data.
- Do not recommend irrelevant keyword stuffing.
- Preserve platform-specific character and policy limits when provided.

## Output

Return keyword groups, placement map, validation needs, and sample natural copy.
