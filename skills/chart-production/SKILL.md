# chart-production

## Purpose

Choose and specify the right chart for business data, reports, and presentations.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, decision question, metric, dimension, time range, comparison, and data grain.
2. Choose chart type: line, bar, stacked bar, scatter, histogram, table, waterfall, funnel, cohort, or heatmap.
3. Prepare chart-ready data and define x, y, series, color, labels, and sort order.
4. Write title, subtitle, annotation, and takeaway.
5. Flag misleading axes, missing denominators, and clutter.

## Required Checks

- Do not generate a chart from missing or unverified data.
- Avoid decorative charts that obscure the question.
- State when only a chart spec is produced rather than an actual image/file.

## Output

Return chart recommendation, chart-ready table, encoding spec, title/subtitle, and caveats.
