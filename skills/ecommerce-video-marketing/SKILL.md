# ecommerce-video-marketing

## Purpose

Plan videos that move buyers from attention to trust to purchase.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, funnel stage, platform, audience, objections, CTA, and available assets.
2. Choose formats: demo, unboxing, tutorial, UGC, comparison, testimonial, live clip, or offer video.
3. Write hooks, scene beats, on-screen text, CTA, thumbnail idea, and caption direction.
4. Map the video set to a launch or content calendar.
5. Define metrics by platform and funnel stage.

## Required Checks

- Do not fabricate testimonials or before-after proof.
- Keep health, finance, safety, and performance claims conservative.
- Do not claim rendered videos without tool evidence.

## Output

Return video strategy, scripts or storyboards, production notes, distribution plan, and measurement checklist.
