# abandoned-cart-winback

## Purpose

Recover purchase intent with helpful reminders, objection handling, and appropriate offers.

## Workflow

1. Identify product, cart context, offer, buyer hesitation, and message channel.
2. Draft sequence: reminder, objection answer, proof, offer, urgency, and final close.
3. Create email subject lines, SMS variants, and onsite prompt copy.
4. Add personalization placeholders and timing assumptions.
5. Mark claims and discount details requiring confirmation.

## Required Checks

- Do not claim sequences were activated.
- Do not invent discounts, inventory, or customer data.
- Keep urgency truthful.

## Output

Return recovery flow, copy variants, timing plan, offer assumptions, and QA checklist.
