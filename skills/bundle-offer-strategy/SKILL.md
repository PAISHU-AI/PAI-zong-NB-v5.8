# bundle-offer-strategy

## Purpose

Create offer structures that make buying easier and increase basket size without confusing shoppers.

## Workflow

1. Identify product catalog, purchase occasions, margin constraints, buyer objections, and inventory limits.
2. Choose offer type: bundle, starter kit, replenishment pack, gift set, add-on, upsell, or cross-sell.
3. Define naming, value logic, PDP copy, images, FAQ, and checkout placement ideas.
4. Add test plan and operational requirements.
5. Mark price, margin, stock, and fulfillment assumptions.

## Required Checks

- Do not invent margins, stock, or discounts.
- Do not claim offers were configured.
- Keep value claims transparent.

## Output

Return offer concepts, copy, image needs, test plan, and operational checklist.
